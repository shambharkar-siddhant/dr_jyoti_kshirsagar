Build a mobile-first, casual, friendly website for a single-doctor DENTAL clinic.

Tone & vibe:
- Warm, approachable, human. Less hospital, more “text your friendly dentist.”
- Microcopy should be short, conversational, and emoji-light (e.g., “Book a quick slot”).
- Use large touch targets, sticky CTAs on mobile, and minimal form fields.

Brand & style:
- Minimalist layout.
- Color palette: soft teal (#11A7A7) + warm sand/peach accent (#F6E7DA) + charcoal text (#222).
- Rounded corners (lg/2xl), subtle shadows, smooth fade/slide transitions.
- Typography: Poppins or Inter for headings, Inter for body. 16–18px base size on mobile.

Pages:
1) Home (primary landing)
2) About
3) Services (cards + detail pages)
4) Book an appointment

Global UX:
- Sticky top bar with logo, “Services”, “About”.
- Footer: clinic address, hours, phone, Instagram link, WhatsApp link, Google Map embed.

HOME PAGE (mobile-first layout):
- Hero: Doctor photo (friendly portrait), name, specialty(Hollywood Smile, No Shaving Teeth, Super Thin, No Injection ,Super Natural).
- Primary CTA row: 
  [Book on WhatsApp] [Call] — large buttons.
- "Smile Transformation" or something better section immediately on Home:
   there will be around 4-6 images of before and after with comparison slider of patients teeths
  Place a tiny note: “We’ll reply on WhatsApp soon.”
- Testimonials (3–5 short reviews) — casual tone, circle avatars.
- Add FAQ section after with 5-6 question and short one liner answers
- Map + quick info:
  - Google Maps embedded (scroll-protected container)
  - Hours table
  - “Directions” link to Google Maps
- Social row: Instagram icon link, WhatsApp icon link.

ABOUT PAGE:
- Friendly intro photo, short bio (education, years of practice, cosmetic focus).
- Approach section: gentle care, painless techniques, honest advice.
- “Book on WhatsApp” CTA.

SERVICES PAGE:
- Cards with large icons and quick benefit copy, e.g.,
  - “Cleaning & Polishing — fresher breath, brighter smile”
  - “Veneers — minimal prep, natural look”
- Clicking a card opens a dedicated service page:
  - Banner image or icon
  - What it is, who it’s for, how long it takes, aftercare tips
  - “Book this service on WhatsApp” button (prefill message with service name)
  - Secondary link back to all services

BOOK PAGE (optional standalone):
- Same 3-field mini-form (Name, WhatsApp, Message) centered, plus a “Book on WhatsApp” instant button.
- Reassurance text: “No spam. We reply fast on WhatsApp.”
- after booking this message will go to patient from doctors number and doctor will reply after that. 

WhatsApp integration specifics:
- Form should compose a friendly prefilled message including Name + Service (if selected) + preferred time.
- Validate phone number for international format; show hint for +91 India numbers.

Map integration:
- Use Google Maps Embed in the footer and on Home near contact.

Technical requirements:
- Next.js (App Router), React, Tailwind CSS.
- Components: Header (sticky), Footer, ServiceCard, TestimonialCard, BookingForm (WhatsApp), MapEmbed, Section wrappers.
- Responsive: prioritize mobile viewports first (375–430px widths).
- Performance: lazy-load map and images; use next/image.
- Add robots.txt, sitemap, and basic SEO tags for clinic name + location.
- Create /api/notify route (stub) that sends an email copy of the request to the doctor (in case WhatsApp isn’t opened by the user). Use environment variables for email target.
- imp - it will be hosted on vercel so make it static. 

Copywriting (placeholders to be replaced later):
- Clinic name, area, and city in headings and metadata.
- Short blurbs per service (1–2 sentences, friendly).
- 3–5 short testimonials (first name, short quote).

Accessibility:
- High-contrast buttons, proper labels, keyboard focus states, aria labels on icons.
- Avoid autoplay media. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.

Deliverables:
- All pages with demo content & images/placeholders.
- Working WhatsApp Click-to-Chat from the booking form and service CTAs.
- Footer with contact details, Instagram link, WhatsApp link, and Google Map embed.
- Minimal backend /api/notify (email stub) for graceful fallback.

Polish:
- Subtle micro-animations (card hover lift, button press).
- Smooth scroll to booking block from any “Book” button.
- Keep everything friendly, clear, and fast on mobile.
